Account Management

Delete Your Account

NIRVO AI — AI English Speaking Coach by NIR Academy

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All speaking practice session scores and history
  • All chat transcripts with NIRVO AI
  • Your progress data and achievements
  • Your coaching preferences and focus settings

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to info@niracademy.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your NIRVO AI account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email when your account has been deleted

Data Retention

Upon deletion:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted from our database immediately
  • No data is retained after deletion
  • Anonymized, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for service improvement
