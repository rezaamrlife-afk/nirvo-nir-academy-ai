Privacy Policy

NIRVO AI — AI English Speaking Coach by NIR Academy

Last updated: June 2026

NIRVO AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Who We Are

NIRVO AI is an AI-powered English speaking coach developed by NIR Academy, founded by Dr. Niki Majidi. Our application is available at nirvo-nir-academy-ai.vercel.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Email address and name when you register
  • Practice data: Speaking session scores (pronunciation, fluency, grammar, vocabulary)
  • Transcripts: Text transcriptions of your speaking practice sessions
  • Usage data: Session duration, topic selections, and practice frequency
  • Chat history: Conversation logs with NIRVO AI (stored per session only)

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not record or store audio files of your voice
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized speaking coaching and feedback
  • To track your progress over time and identify areas for improvement
  • To generate your "Today's Focus" coaching recommendations
  • To maintain your account and session history
  • To improve the quality of our AI coaching system

5. Third-Party Services

NIRVO AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Secure database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Groq AI — AI language model for generating coaching feedback (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — Hosting and deployment (Privacy Policy)
  • Web Speech API — Browser-based speech recognition (processed locally on your device)

Your voice is processed locally on your device by the Web Speech API. Audio is never sent to our servers.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on Supabase servers in the EU (eu-west-1)
  • Your data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • API keys and credentials are never exposed to users

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your practice data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Children's Privacy

NIRVO AI is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Microphone Permission

NIRVO AI requests access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of speech recognition during practice sessions. Microphone access is:

  • Only activated when you press the microphone button
  • Processed locally by your browser's Web Speech API
  • Never recorded or transmitted to our servers
  • Revocable at any time through your browser or device settings

11. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store:

  • Your name and email (for session persistence)
  • Your current coaching focus area

This data is stored locally on your device and is cleared when you sign out.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of NIRVO AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
