Privacy Policy

NIRVO AI — AI English Speaking Coach by NIR Academy

Last updated: June 2026

NIRVO AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

NIR Academy
Founded by Dr. Niki Majidi — TESOL & ELT Training
Email: info@niracademy.com

2. Who We Are

NIRVO AI is an AI-powered English speaking coach developed by NIR Academy. Our application is available at nirvo-nir-academy-ai.vercel.app.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data to provide and improve the services you request, maintain your account, and fulfill our contractual obligations to you. Where required by law, we rely on your consent or our legitimate interests in providing an effective educational service.

4. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Email address and name when you register
  • Practice data: Speaking session scores (pronunciation, fluency, grammar, vocabulary)
  • Transcripts: Text transcriptions of your speaking practice sessions
  • Usage data: Session duration, topic selections, and practice frequency
  • Chat history: Conversation logs with NIRVO AI (stored per session only)

5. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store audio recordings of your voice on our servers
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

6. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized speaking coaching and feedback
  • To track your progress over time and identify areas for improvement
  • To generate your "Today's Focus" coaching recommendations
  • To maintain your account and session history
  • To improve the quality of our AI coaching system

7. Third-Party Services

NIRVO AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Secure database and authentication · Privacy Policy
  • Groq AI — AI language model for generating coaching feedback · Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Hosting and deployment · Privacy Policy
  • Web Speech API — Browser-based speech recognition (processed locally on your device by your browser)

Speech recognition is handled by your browser's built-in Web Speech API. NIRVO AI does not store audio recordings on its servers.

8. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on Supabase servers (EU region)
  • Your data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • API keys and credentials are never exposed to users

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at info@niracademy.com.

10. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your personal data where applicable under law

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@niracademy.com.

11. Children's Privacy

NIRVO AI is intended for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@niracademy.com.

12. Microphone Permission

NIRVO AI requests access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of speech recognition during practice sessions. Microphone access is:

  • Only activated when you press the microphone button
  • Processed locally by your browser's Web Speech API
  • Not recorded or transmitted to NIRVO AI servers
  • Revocable at any time through your browser or device settings

13. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store:

  • Your name and email (for session persistence)
  • Your current coaching focus area

This data is stored locally on your device and is cleared when you sign out.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of NIRVO AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
